Sports

What Are Sports?

Sports are physical activities that involve skill, competition, and teamwork. They promote physical fitness, mental strength, and social interaction.

Benefits of Sports

Playing sports improves health, builds confidence, teaches discipline, and encourages teamwork and leadership.

Popular Sports

Football, basketball, athletics, cricket, and volleyball are among the most popular sports enjoyed worldwide.
