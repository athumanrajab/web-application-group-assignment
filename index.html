Welcome to the Community Information & Services Portal

The Community Information & Services Portal (CIS Portal) is a centralized web platform designed to provide community members with easy access to essential services, information, and opportunities available within the community.

Purpose of the Portal

This portal aims to bridge the information gap by bringing together important community services such as health, education, security, employment opportunities, local businesses, and cultural activities into one accessible website.

What You Can Find Here

Through this platform, users can:

  • Access health and emergency service information
  • Explore educational institutions and learning opportunities
  • Stay informed about security and emergency contacts
  • Find jobs, internships, and volunteer opportunities
  • Discover local businesses and community events
  • Learn about tourism, culture, and heritage

How to Use the Website

Use the navigation menu at the top of the page to explore different sections of the portal. Each page provides detailed and relevant information to support community awareness and development.
