The Public Services Sector focuses on government and community-supported services that are essential for daily life. These services support the community but are separate from health, education, and security sectors.

Key Public Services

Water Supply Services
Access to clean and reliable water for households and businesses.
Electricity Services (e.g. TANESCO)
Power supply information, outage updates, and billing support.
Transportation Services
Public transport information, roads, and local travel services.
Local Government Offices
Information on offices, permits, community administration, and contacts.
Community Support Programs
Social welfare programs, youth initiatives, and community development support.

Why This Sector Matters

  • Covers services everyone uses daily
